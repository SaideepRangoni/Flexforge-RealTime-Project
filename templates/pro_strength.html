Pro Strength Training Routine

Overview

As a pro, you need to focus on maximum strength gains with advanced techniques like progressive overload, compound lifts, and power exercises. This plan is designed for serious lifters looking to push their limits.

Exercises

  • Squat (Heavy): 5 sets of 6 reps
  • Deadlifts: 5 sets of 6 reps
  • Clean and Jerk: 4 sets of 5 reps
  • Snatch: 4 sets of 5 reps
  • Barbell Rows: 5 sets of 8 reps

Sample Weekly Plan

Day 1: Squats, Deadlifts, Clean and Jerk

Day 2: Snatch, Barbell Rows, Core

Day 3: Rest or light cardio

Day 4: Squats, Deadlifts, Snatch

Day 5: Clean and Jerk, Barbell Rows

Day 6: Rest or active recovery

Day 7: Full-body workout focusing on compound lifts
