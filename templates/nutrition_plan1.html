Weekly Nutrition Plan for Strength Training

Day 1 (Monday) - High Protein Day

Breakfast:

Scrambled eggs (3 whole eggs, 2 egg whites), Oats with almond butter and banana, A glass of milk

Snack:

Greek yogurt with mixed nuts

Lunch:

Grilled chicken breast, Brown rice, Steamed broccoli

Snack:

Protein shake, Apple

Dinner:

Salmon, Quinoa, Mixed salad with olive oil dressing

Day 2 (Tuesday) - Moderate Carb Day

Breakfast:

Whole grain toast with avocado and poached eggs, Orange juice

Snack:

Cottage cheese with cucumber slices

Lunch:

Turkey breast, Sweet potato, Spinach and kale salad

Snack:

Hummus with carrot sticks

Dinner:

Beef stir-fry with veggies, Wild rice

Day 3 (Wednesday) - Balanced Macro Day

Breakfast:

Smoothie (spinach, banana, protein powder, almond milk, chia seeds), Boiled eggs

Snack:

Handful of almonds and a pear

Lunch:

Grilled chicken wrap with whole wheat tortilla, Lettuce, tomato, and avocado

Snack:

Hard-boiled eggs, Cherry tomatoes

Dinner:

Grilled shrimp, Whole grain pasta, Asparagus

Day 4 (Thursday) - Lower Carb Day

Breakfast:

Omelet with spinach, onions, and bell peppers, Green tea

Snack:

Protein bar

Lunch:

Grilled chicken thighs, Cauliflower rice, Zucchini

Snack:

Almonds and dark chocolate (85% cocoa)

Dinner:

Baked cod, Mixed green salad with balsamic vinaigrette

Day 5 (Friday) - High Protein Day

Breakfast:

Protein pancakes (made with oats, eggs, and protein powder), Blueberries

Snack:

Greek yogurt with chia seeds and honey

Lunch:

Tuna salad with olive oil dressing, Whole grain crackers

Snack:

Mixed nuts

Dinner:

Grilled steak, Mashed sweet potato, Green beans

Day 6 (Saturday) - Balanced Day

Breakfast:

Whole grain toast with peanut butter and banana slices, Skimmed milk

Snack:

Boiled eggs

Lunch:

Grilled chicken, Couscous, Roasted vegetables

Snack:

Protein shake

Dinner:

Turkey meatballs, Zucchini noodles, Tomato sauce

Day 7 (Sunday) - Rest/Recovery Day

Breakfast:

Smoothie (strawberry, Greek yogurt, flax seeds, almond milk)

Snack:

Handful of mixed nuts and dried fruit

Lunch:

Baked salmon, Quinoa salad

Snack:

Cottage cheese

Dinner:

Roast chicken, Brown rice, Steamed broccoli
