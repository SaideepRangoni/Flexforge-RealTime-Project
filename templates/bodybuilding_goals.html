Goal Setting and Planning

1. Defining Your Goals

Before you begin your bodybuilding journey, it's important to set clear goals. Determine if your aim is to build muscle mass, increase strength, or improve overall body composition.

Key Points:

  • Set specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals.
  • Examples: "Increase muscle mass by 5kg in 6 months" or "Improve bench press strength by 20% in 3 months."
2. Planning Your Routine

Once your goals are set, plan a workout routine that focuses on all major muscle groups. Incorporate both compound and isolation exercises into your routine.

Workout Tips:

  • Plan your workouts 3-5 times per week, depending on your fitness level and goals.
  • Ensure your plan includes exercises for your chest, back, legs, shoulders, arms, and core.
  • Start each workout with compound exercises (like squats and bench presses) to target multiple muscle groups.
