Principles of Strength Training

Principle 1: Overload

To build strength, you need to overload your muscles by progressively increasing the weight you lift.

Principle 2: Specificity

Train specific muscles for the goals you want to achieve. Focus on the muscle group you aim to improve.

Principle 3: Recovery

Ensure that you give your muscles time to recover between sessions to avoid injury and promote growth.
