Weekly Nutritional Diet for Powerlifting

Monday

Breakfast: Oatmeal with protein powder, banana, and almonds.

Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and steamed vegetables.

Dinner: Baked salmon with sweet potatoes and broccoli.

Tuesday

Breakfast: Greek yogurt with mixed berries and honey.

Lunch: Turkey wrap with spinach, avocado, and whole wheat tortilla.

Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with brown rice and bell peppers.

Wednesday

Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, protein powder, and mixed fruits.

Lunch: Beef steak with mashed potatoes and green beans.

Dinner: Lentil soup with whole grain bread.

Thursday

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and avocado on whole grain toast.

Lunch: Chicken and vegetable stir-fry with quinoa.

Dinner: Grilled shrimp with couscous and mixed vegetables.

Friday

Breakfast: Protein pancakes with fresh berries and a side of Greek yogurt.

Lunch: Tuna salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and olives.

Dinner: Spaghetti with lean ground beef and a side salad.

Saturday

Breakfast: Cottage cheese with pineapple and a handful of walnuts.

Lunch: Chicken Caesar salad with whole grain croutons.

Dinner: Baked cod with roasted vegetables and a quinoa salad.

Sunday

Breakfast: Chia pudding with almond milk and fresh fruit.

Lunch: Turkey burger with a side of sweet potato fries.

Dinner: Stuffed bell peppers with brown rice and black beans.
