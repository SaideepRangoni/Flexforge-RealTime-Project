Understanding Cardiovascular Training

What is Cardiovascular Training?

Cardiovascular training involves exercises that increase your heart rate and improve the efficiency of your cardiovascular system, including your heart and lungs. This type of training strengthens your heart and lungs, enhancing your overall fitness.

Benefits of Cardiovascular Training

  • Improves heart health and circulation
  • Increases lung capacity and oxygen flow
  • Aids in weight management through calorie burning
  • Boosts stamina and endurance
  • Enhances mental health and reduces stress

Types of Cardiovascular Exercises

Common cardiovascular exercises include running, cycling, swimming, and using machines such as treadmills, ellipticals, and stationary bikes.
