Weekly Nutritional Diet for Cardiovascular Training

Monday

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with berries and almonds
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed broccoli
  • Snacks: Apple slices with peanut butter

Tuesday

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt with honey and granola
  • Lunch: Turkey wrap with whole grain tortilla and veggies
  • Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables and brown rice
  • Snacks: Carrot sticks with hummus

Wednesday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, and protein powder
  • Lunch: Quinoa and black bean salad with avocado
  • Dinner: Grilled shrimp with sweet potato and asparagus
  • Snacks: Mixed nuts

Thursday

  • Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado and poached egg
  • Lunch: Lentil soup with a side of mixed greens
  • Dinner: Baked chicken breast with roasted vegetables
  • Snacks: Greek yogurt with fresh fruit

Friday

  • Breakfast: Chia seed pudding with almond milk and berries
  • Lunch: Tuna salad with mixed greens and a lemon vinaigrette
  • Dinner: Beef stir-fry with bell peppers and brown rice
  • Snacks: Sliced cucumber with tzatziki

Saturday

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole grain toast
  • Lunch: Chicken and vegetable kebabs with a side salad
  • Dinner: Grilled tilapia with roasted sweet potatoes and green beans
  • Snacks: A handful of almonds

Sunday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie bowl with banana, spinach, and chia seeds
  • Lunch: Turkey and vegetable stew
  • Dinner: Stuffed bell peppers with quinoa and black beans
  • Snacks: Fresh fruit salad
