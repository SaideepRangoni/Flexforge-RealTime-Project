Olympic Weightlifting Guide

1. Understanding Olympic Weightlifting

Olympic weightlifting involves two main lifts: the snatch and the clean and jerk. It focuses on explosive power, technique, and strength.

Goals: Improve overall strength, power, speed, and coordination while enhancing athletic performance.

Learn More
2. The Snatch

Technique: Lift the barbell from the ground to overhead in one motion using strength, speed, and technique.

Steps: Setup, First Pull, Transition, Second Pull, Catch, and Stand Up.

Learn More
3. The Clean and Jerk

Technique: Lift the barbell to the shoulders (clean) and then press it overhead (jerk) in two distinct phases.

Steps: Setup, First Pull, Transition, Second Pull, Catch, and Drive.

Learn More
4. Warm-Up and Mobility

Warm up with cardio and dynamic stretches, and focus on mobility for shoulders, hips, and ankles.

Learn More
5. Safety and Progression

Ensure proper form, use appropriate weights, and progress gradually. Consider coaching for guided improvement.
