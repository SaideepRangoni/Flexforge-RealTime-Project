Types of Circuit Training

Strength Circuits

Description: Strength circuits focus on building muscle strength through resistance exercises.

Examples: Exercises such as dumbbell curls, lunges, push-ups, and squats are commonly included.

Cardio Circuits

Description: Cardio circuits are designed to enhance cardiovascular fitness by incorporating high-intensity cardio exercises.

Examples: Activities like jumping jacks, running, burpees, and jump rope are often used.

Mixed Circuits

Description: Mixed circuits combine both strength and cardio exercises for a well-rounded workout.

Examples: A session might include bodyweight exercises like squats and push-ups along with cardio bursts like high knees or mountain climbers.
