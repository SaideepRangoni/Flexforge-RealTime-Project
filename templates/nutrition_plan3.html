Weekly Nutrition Plan for Bodybuilding Training

Monday

Breakfast
Oatmeal with protein powder, berries, and nuts
Lunch
Grilled chicken breast, quinoa, and steamed vegetables
Dinner
Salmon, sweet potato, and green salad

Tuesday

Breakfast
Greek yogurt with honey and granola
Lunch
Turkey sandwich with whole grain bread and mixed greens
Dinner
Beef stir-fry with brown rice and mixed vegetables

Wednesday

Breakfast
Smoothie with spinach, banana, protein powder, and almond milk
Lunch
Chicken and avocado salad with a side of fruit
Dinner
Tuna steak with asparagus and wild rice

Thursday

Breakfast
Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole grain toast
Lunch
Grilled shrimp with couscous and mixed vegetables
Dinner
Pork loin with roasted potatoes and green beans

Friday

Breakfast
Chia seed pudding with almond butter and fruit
Lunch
Baked chicken thighs with barley and broccoli
Dinner
Grilled fish tacos with avocado and salsa

Saturday

Breakfast
Protein pancakes with a side of mixed berries
Lunch
Lentil soup with a side of whole grain bread
Dinner
Stuffed bell peppers with ground turkey and quinoa

Sunday

Breakfast
Overnight oats with almond milk, chia seeds, and fruit
Lunch
Salmon salad with mixed greens, avocado, and nuts
Dinner
Beef stew with vegetables and a side of whole grain rice
