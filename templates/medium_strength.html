Intermediate Strength Training Routine

Overview

You've built a strong foundation, and now it's time to challenge your muscles with compound lifts and progressive overload. Follow this routine for 6-8 weeks to increase strength and build muscle.

Exercises

  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 8 reps (assisted if needed)
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps (each leg)
  • Overhead Press: 4 sets of 8 reps

Sample Weekly Plan

Day 1: Deadlifts, Bench Press, Pull-ups

Day 2: Lunges, Overhead Press, Core Exercises

Day 3: Rest or light cardio

Day 4: Deadlifts, Lunges, Pull-ups

Day 5: Bench Press, Overhead Press

Day 6: Rest or active recovery (stretching, yoga)

Day 7: Full-body workout (repeat a mix of exercises from Day 1 and Day 2)
