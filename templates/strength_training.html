Strength Training Routine

Beginner Strength Training

This routine is designed for those who are new to strength training. It focuses on building a foundation of strength with basic exercises.

  • Exercises: Squats, Push-ups, Dumbbell Rows, Planks.
  • Frequency: 3 times per week.
  • Reps: 2-3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  • Rest: 1 minute between sets.

Medium (Intermediate) Strength Training

This routine is for those who have built a solid base and want to continue improving strength and muscle size.

  • Exercises: Deadlifts, Bench Press, Pull-ups, Lunges.
  • Frequency: 4 times per week.
  • Reps: 3-4 sets of 8-10 reps.
  • Rest: 1-2 minutes between sets.

Pro Strength Training

Designed for advanced trainees looking to maximize strength gains and improve muscle definition.

  • Exercises: Squat Variations, Clean and Jerk, Snatch, Weighted Dips, Barbell Rows.
  • Frequency: 5-6 times per week.
  • Reps: 4-5 sets of 6-8 reps.
  • Rest: 2-3 minutes between sets.
