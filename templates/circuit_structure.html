Structure of a Circuit

Components of a Circuit

Circuit training typically includes a variety of exercises that focus on different muscle groups and fitness aspects.

  • Bodyweight Exercises: Push-ups, squats, lunges, etc.
  • Resistance Training: Dumbbell curls, deadlifts, lunges, etc.
  • Cardiovascular Activities: Jump rope, burpees, high knees, etc.

Each exercise targets different muscle groups to provide a balanced, full-body workout.

Sequence of a Circuit

Exercises are typically arranged in a specific sequence, and participants move from one exercise to the next with minimal rest between them. A standard circuit is repeated multiple times depending on the individual's fitness level and workout goal.

  • Time per Station: 30 seconds to 1 minute per exercise.
  • Rest Periods: Minimal rest (usually 15-30 seconds) between exercises.
  • Repetition: Complete the circuit 3-4 times for optimal results.

This approach allows for a high-intensity, efficient workout that challenges both the cardiovascular system and muscles.
