Beginner Strength Training Routine

Overview

This plan is designed for those new to strength training. It focuses on foundational exercises that build strength, flexibility, and endurance. Stick to this routine for 4-6 weeks before progressing to the intermediate level.

Exercises

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 10 reps (each arm)
  • Planks: Hold for 30 seconds, 3 times
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 15 reps

Sample Weekly Plan

Day 1: Squats, Push-ups, Planks

Day 2: Dumbbell Rows, Glute Bridges

Day 3: Squats, Push-ups, Planks

Day 4: Rest

Day 5: Full-body workout (repeat exercises from Day 1 and 2)

Day 6: Rest or light activity (walking, stretching)

Day 7: Rest
