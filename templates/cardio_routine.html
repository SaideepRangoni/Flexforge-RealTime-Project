Designing a Cardio Workout Routine

Warm-Up

Begin with 5-10 minutes of low-intensity cardio to prepare your body and reduce the risk of injury. This can include brisk walking, slow jogging, or cycling at a low pace.

Workout Duration

Aim for at least 20-30 minutes of moderate to vigorous cardio exercise. You can break it down into intervals, or maintain a steady pace depending on your fitness goals.

Intensity Levels

Incorporate different intensity levels, such as:

  • Steady-state cardio: Maintaining a consistent pace throughout your workout.
  • Interval training: Alternating between high-intensity bursts and low-intensity recovery periods.

Cool-Down

End with 5-10 minutes of low-intensity cardio and stretching to gradually lower your heart rate and prevent muscle stiffness. Stretch the muscles used in your workout to aid in recovery.
